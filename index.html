The Studio

Not chasing scale.
Chasing correctness.

Both products exist because tools that were supposed to help had exits built in. An ignore button on your screen time app. An ingredient list engineered to hide what's inside. We build without those. Every Rewyr block was set by you when you were thinking clearly. Every Labelyze flag cites the exact regulation that triggered it. We enforce. We don't decide.

Every Claim Has a Source

We don't flag things without evidence. Every Labelyze alert cites a regulation. Every Rewyr block was set by you.

04
Built for India, Not Adapted for It

Both products were designed for this market from day one. Not ported from somewhere else and made to fit.
